A little bit about myself

After growing up in the world of ballet I pursued a career as a music publicist, but a life of pitching journalists and red carpets left me feeling unfulfilled and I landed in the tech field. Now I have a passion for introducing women just like myself to this industry and showing them that they too can succeed. Outside of work, I enjoy playing with my golden retriever mix, going to concerts and seeing a ballet whenever I can.
